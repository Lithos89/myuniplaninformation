How To Use

The basic feature of My Uni Plan is to deconstruct complicated degree structures like the Bachelor of Commerce into a simple and elegant diagram. By hovering over courses (clicking not yet added), you can see every course required to take a certain course. At the moment, only singular required courses are properly higlighted. Choice requirements as indicated by the dashed purple line indicate that you have a choice as to which prerequisite requirement to fill. The red solid line indicates neseccary prerequisites and when highlighted is represented in blue.

Courses are highlighted as well as their prerequisite courses when you hover over a course box. These course boxes are movable so you can orient the diagram in any way you like!

Clicking a button to zoom in and out inside is currently not supported yet. This means that you will need to do this on your inside the browser. Since each browser is different, follow the steps for your specific browser.

  • Chrome: https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/96810?co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop&hl=en
  • Safari: https://support.apple.com/en-ca/guide/safari/ibrw1068/mac
  • Firefox: https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/font-size-and-zoom-increase-size-of-web-pages
  • Edge: https://thegeekpage.com/zoom-in-zoom-out-in-edge-web-browser/

My Uni Plan is still in the development phase so not everything works as intended.
